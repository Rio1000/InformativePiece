What is Dyslexia?

Dyslexia is a specific learning difficulty that primarily affects reading and writing. It is often characterized by difficulties in phonological processing, spelling, and rapid visual-verbal responding.

Common Symptoms of Dyslexia

  • Difficulty with reading and spelling
  • Struggling to recognize letters or words
  • Problems with sequencing information
  • Slow reading speed or understanding text
  • Difficulty with organization

Support for Dyslexia

If you or someone you know is dealing with dyslexia, there are resources and strategies that can help. Here are a few:

  • Educational therapy and specialized tutoring
  • Assistive technologies like text-to-speech software
  • Creating a supportive learning environment
  • Breaking tasks into smaller, manageable steps
